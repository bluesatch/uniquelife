our mission

Since 2015, Unique Life Ministries, Inc., has been dedicated to promoting Advancement in life-enhancing areas including Health, Fitness, and Career Development for youth and adults.

our goal

Unique Life Ministries, Inc. envisions the creation of the ‘Cause Center’. A center dedicated to the holistic development of youth and adults using unique methods, classes and activities that promote healthier lifestyles.

We envision healthier people achieving their goals by working on causes important to them.

our team

Carla Nichols

Carla M Nichols serves as the President of Unique Life Ministries, Inc. She hails from Tuscaloosa, Alabama. She has been a native of Mississippi for 15 years. Carla is married to Fredrick Nichols and together they have 3 children. She comes with 13 years of Human Resources experience in the retail and automotive industries. Carla’s favorite past time is doing choral workshops for local churches. In her downtime she enjoys watching re-runs of her favorite shows and hanging out with her family.

Daisy Jackson

Daisy Jackson serves as Vice President of Unique Life Ministries, Inc. She is originally from Sharon, Mississippi and now resides in Canton, MS. She has 3 children and 7 grandchildren. Daisy, or “Aunt Daisy” as she’s affectionally know, has 38 years of retail/customer service experience. She enjoys traveling and spending time with her family. She is also known in her church family as the “one who gives the best hugs”.

Cassandra Evans-Brown

Cassandra Evans-Brown serves as Secretary of Unique Life Ministries. She brings more than 20 years of experience in paralegal, human resources and administration work in the non-profit, private and federal sectors. Most recently, she handled human resources for a defense aerospace company that focused on aircraft operation support. A native of Yazoo City, Mississippi, she traveled the world while serving a rewarding 13 years in the United States Army. Cassandra is happily married to Herbert and they have 2 children, Josiah and Y’Sonni. She enjoys event planning and cooking.

Reo Cash

Reo Cash serves as Treasurer of Unique Life Ministries, Inc. She is married to her college sweetheart, Antoine, and they have 5 sons: KiJuan, KeyShaun, Ahmoni, Kannon and Ar’riel. Originally from Natchez, Mississippi, she currently resides in Madison, Mississippi. Reo is an investment account with 14 years of professional accounting experience. She is currently responsible for accounting for a diverse $14 billion investment portfolio. In her spare time Reo enjoys exercising and baking.

Angela Y. Carson

Angela Y. Carson serves as the Consultant for Unique Life Ministries, Inc. She has over 20 years of management and training experience in the community development, business, military and educational fields. She founded Carson Consulting Services, LLC in 2001 to help organizations, corporations and government agencies increase their effectiveness through training and consultation. She provides assistance in a number of capacity-building areas including job readiness, nonprofit management, strategic planning, grant writing, soft skills, leadership development, resource development, and more. As a John Maxwell Certified Trainer and Coach, she trains and coaches individuals and organizations to improve their leadership abilities.
